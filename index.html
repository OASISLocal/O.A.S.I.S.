Everything runs on your machine. You don't need Docker, Python, or a terminal. Just the installer.

Seven local tools: bash (run commands), file (read/write files), edit (search and replace), regex (search code), web_search (DuckDuckGo), memory (persistent key-value storage), and mcp (basic MCP client).

Plus the ControllerTool lets you open, close, and control any Windows app with 5 automatic fallback levels.

When the model is unsure, the system forces it to use internet_search before answering. Three layers of protection:

1. Prompt: Dynamic instructions before every message push the model to use tools when in doubt.
2. Post-response detection: If the model generates text like "according to search results" without having actually searched, the system detects the hallucination, runs internet_search automatically, and retries.
3. Preserved history: Retries keep the full conversation context so the model learns from its mistake.

A GPU speeds things up significantly but is not required. On a modern CPU, a 7–8B model generates 5–15 tokens/second — fast enough for conversation. If you have an NVIDIA GPU, O.A.S.I.S. offloads layers automatically.

The recommended starting point is 8 GB RAM running deepseek-r1:8b in CPU mode.

Local AI explained

Why choose local AI
over Cloud providers?

The difference isn't just technical — it changes who's in control of your data, your costs, and your experience.

Privacy you can actually trust

Cloud AI services process your prompts on their servers. That means your conversations, documents, and ideas travel over the internet and can be stored, reviewed, or used to train future models. With a local AI like O.A.S.I.S., inference happens entirely on your hardware. Your prompts never leave your machine — not even for a millisecond. It works in airplane mode permanently.

Zero ongoing cost

Subscription plans for cloud AI typically cost $20–$200 per month, and prices often increase as features improve. With O.A.S.I.S., you download it once and it's yours forever. You choose and download the models yourself (many are free and open source). No credit card, no API key, no usage caps. The only cost is your own hardware — which you already own.

Works with Llama, Mistral, DeepSeek and more

O.A.S.I.S. is compatible with any model in GGUF format and integrates with Ollama out of the box. That includes DeepSeek R1, Llama 3.2, Mistral 7B, Phi-3 Mini, Qwen, Gemma, and dozens of others. You're not locked into one provider's model — you can switch, experiment, and update as the open-source AI ecosystem evolves.

Real tools, not just chat

Most local AI frontends are thin wrappers around a chat API. O.A.S.I.S. is different: it's an agentic system with 7 built-in tools — bash, file editing, web search, app control, and more. When you ask it something it doesn't know, it searches the web automatically instead of making something up. When you ask it to open an app or edit a file, it actually does it.
